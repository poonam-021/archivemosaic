Our Mission

Our mission is to digitally archive and preserve cultural data from across India, ensuring its accessibility for future generations. Through digital tools, we strive to protect cultural heritage and make it available globally for educational and research purposes.

Our Vision

We envision a world where all cultural data is digitized and easily accessible to anyone, anywhere. By making cultural knowledge accessible, we aim to foster greater understanding, appreciation, and celebration of humanity's diverse histories.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We maintain the authenticity and accuracy of cultural data during its digital preservation process.
  • Accessibility: We believe in making cultural data available to all, ensuring no heritage is excluded from the digital era.
  • Inclusivity: We celebrate the richness of all cultures, ensuring that no culture, language, or tradition is overlooked.
  • Innovation: We continuously embrace cutting-edge technologies and innovative methods to preserve, protect, and share cultural knowledge.

Website Overview

This Website is dedicated to digitalizing cultural data—whether it's historical documents, oral histories, or traditional artifacts. By creating digital archives, we help safeguard cultural heritage from physical decay while promoting its global dissemination for educational and research purposes.

Uploading – A Basic Guide

If you would like to do so now, click on this link: Free account to be redirected.

What files can I upload?

By having a free account, you will be able to upload files such as music and audio recordings; videos, software and images, and much more.

What file formats should I use to upload?

To ensure quality and consistency, our platform currently supports uploads in the following formats: PDF, JPEG, MP3, DOCX, and IMG. These formats help us maintain accessibility and preserve cultural content effectively.

Why Digital Archiving?

Digital archiving ensures long-term preservation and accessibility of cultural heritage. It safeguards against physical degradation and provides an educational bridge across generations, communities, and continents.
